Moldy grain can be lethal. Be sure to clean all the old grain out of your bin or barrel before refilling it.

When feeding, don't dump new grain on old. Note that the horse did not eat, especially if it is unusual. That could be a sign the horse is getting sick or needs his meds managed differently. Toss what was not eaten. It is a good idea to scrub feed tubs and water buckets with dish soap once a week.
