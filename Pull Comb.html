I NEVER use these. They cut my hands, make a terrible sound when they drop and teach horses to hate pulling.

If you need to wrap hair around the comb, you are pulling too much. Plus, the wrapping allows horses to get anxious.

I use a people comb, take just a few strands and simply press my thumb against the spine of the comb. Its easy, low impact and agreeable. Here are more tips and photos for pulling peacefully.
