DON'T CUT Hair

Braiding, it is very tempting. But, if you cut, it will be worse next time. Truth is, the most important part of the braid job is the bottomline. If that's straight, your eye is drawn to it, so the ]ispies become insignificant. Why? If the line is straight, that is what your eye sees. So, wet the mane well to braid it, so hair locks in better. Then, resist temptation.
