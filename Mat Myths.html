Mats can be beneficial, but do not solve all equine issues. In fact, they can promote problems. However, with some awareness, you can optimize mats’ utility.

His feet may slide too wide and strain. Have you ever known a horse to wake up lame? Sometimes they slip getting up on mats or get cast against the wall. Plenty of bedding can solve the sliding.
