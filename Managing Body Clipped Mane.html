Don't cut the mane. If there was a slip of the clippers, hang on. The wispies in this pic could have twisted up or would have the next week.

Always braid the mane when wet, top and bottom. Use the back of your comb as a sweat scraper, so drops of water are not distracting to your horse. Wet hair stretches and folds in better. To grab those short pieces, give a twist to the sections as you grab them at the top. That will tuck the short hair under the rest.
