KEEP 'EM DRINKING!

Horses need to be well hydrated to avert illness.

KEEP IT FRESH AND EASY.

When it's hot, change water twice a day so it stays yummy. Let them drink before bathing and/or grooming. When traveling, offer them water with apple juice or Denji in it. They'll happily slurp it up. Then, toss the remaining water and rinse bucket as it would ferment.
